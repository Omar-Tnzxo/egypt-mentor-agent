# Skill: Growth

Use this skill when the user asks how to grow a project, product, business, career path, audience, or revenue stream.

## Goal

Help the agent think about growth as a system, not as a fixed playbook.
The right growth move depends on the current bottleneck.

## Core rule

Growth is not always about getting more users.
Sometimes the real growth constraint is:

- weak retention
- unclear value
- wrong target actor
- weak monetization
- low trust
- low conversion
- poor execution capacity
- channel mismatch

Do not recommend growth tactics before identifying what kind of growth is actually blocked.

## Growth lens types

The agent should determine which type of growth matters most right now:

- user growth
- activation growth
- retention growth
- conversion growth
- revenue growth
- account growth
- trust growth
- distribution growth

## Growth diagnosis

Always inspect:

- what "growth" means in this case
- where users or customers are leaking
- what stage of the funnel is weakest
- whether the offer is strong enough for more traffic
- whether the current channel matches the target actor
- whether the user has enough operational capacity to support growth

## Core tests

### 1. Bottleneck test

Where is the biggest leak or constraint right now?

### 2. Readiness test

If more people arrived today, would the current product, offer, or workflow convert and retain them well enough?

### 3. Channel test

Is the current path for reaching people appropriate for the actor, market, and trust level involved?

### 4. Capacity test

Can the user or team actually handle more demand if growth starts working?

### 5. Evidence test

What numbers prove that growth is the right priority now instead of validation, monetization, or product correction?

## Common growth mistakes

Avoid:

- trying to grow a weak offer
- buying attention before proving value
- scaling traffic when retention is poor
- treating signups as success
- adding channels before fixing the main leak
- chasing vanity growth that does not improve business health

## If growth is not the right priority

The agent should say so clearly and redirect to the real bottleneck.
Growth should not be used as a distraction from a more fundamental problem.

## Output requirements

When this skill is active, include:

- what kind of growth matters most right now
- the main growth bottleneck
- why growth is or is not the current priority
- the highest-leverage growth move
- what must not be worked on yet
- what evidence would prove the growth decision was correct
