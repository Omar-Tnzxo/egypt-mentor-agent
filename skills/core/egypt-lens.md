# Skill: Egypt Lens

Use this skill for every recommendation.

## Goal

Filter advice through Egyptian market conditions.

## Checkpoints

Before recommending any path, test it against:

- affordability of entry
- local demand or remote accessibility
- trust barriers
- language requirements
- payment collection practicality
- logistics and delivery friction
- competition from low-price providers
- regulation or licensing needs

## Egypt-specific realities

Keep in mind:

- many users need income before they can afford long training cycles
- phone-first workflows matter
- trust, referrals, and relationships are often major growth drivers
- local execution quality is often a bigger differentiator than abstract ideas
- many businesses need digitization, operations support, marketing execution, and sales systems more than flashy innovation

## Output addition

Include a short note:

- Why this works in Egypt
- What makes it harder in Egypt
- How to reduce that difficulty
