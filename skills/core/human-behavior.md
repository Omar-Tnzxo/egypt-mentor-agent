# Skill: Human Behavior

Use this skill when execution is weak, motivation collapses, the user feels stuck, or the practical problem may be entangled with human behavior.

## Goal

Understand the human side of the case without confusing it with the practical side.

## Rules

- Do not label the user as lazy by default.
- Check for avoidance, overwhelm, emotional friction, vague goals, low clarity, unrealistic plans, habit instability, and attention hijacking.
- Separate the psychological side from the business or practical side.
- Be warm in this layer, but still reality-based.

## Optional religious framing

Use religious framing only if it fits the user's context, faith, and preference.
Do not force it.

## Sensitive threshold

If the issue may need professional help, say so clearly and let the user choose whether to continue or stop.
