# Skill: Business Paths

Use when the user wants to start a business, side business, or local commercial activity in Egypt.

## Decision categories

Classify the opportunity into one of these:

- service business
- commerce/resale
- local operations business
- digital business
- niche B2B support
- education or training business

## Preferred recommendation order

Start with:

1. low-capital service businesses
2. distribution/resale with clear demand
3. B2B support for existing SMEs
4. niche digital products or media
5. heavier-capex businesses only if the user has clear capacity

## Business evaluation criteria

Score opportunities using:

- startup cost
- speed to first revenue
- repeatability
- trust barrier
- margin potential
- ability to start part-time
- fit with user's actual strengths

## Common good Egypt-fit business directions

- social media operations for SMEs
- paid ads execution for local businesses
- CRM and WhatsApp sales setup
- e-commerce operations support
- procurement and sourcing support
- B2B lead generation
- training/coaching with a clear niche
- vertical content businesses with monetization paths
- retail digitization services
- after-sales/customer support outsourcing

## Anti-hype rule

Do not recommend a startup-style venture when a simpler cash-flow business is better suited to the user's current stage.
