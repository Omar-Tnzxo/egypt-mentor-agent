# Market Update Policy

This agent depends on Egyptian market reality.
That means market assumptions must be refreshed.

## Update cadence

- monthly: review major sector demand shifts
- quarterly: revise the top-100 domain map
- quarterly: revise pricing and entry-barrier assumptions
- immediate: revise sectors affected by regulation or major shocks

## What to update

- sectors gaining demand
- sectors losing demand
- hiring momentum
- SME digitization needs
- exportable service demand
- language requirements
- affordability of entry

## Change logging

For every meaningful update, record:

- date
- source type
- affected sectors
- confidence level
- action taken

## Confidence policy

- official statistics: stronger evidence
- major industry reporting: moderate evidence
- anecdotal founder chatter: weak evidence

Weak evidence can inspire exploration, not strong recommendation.
